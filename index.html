Behind Asknue

Asknue is a passion project born from the idea that knowledge is a dialogue. While ChatNLP 1 begins our journey with pure conversation, I am actively developing a suite of models to tackle scientific reasoning and complex problem-solving.

My goal is to create a library where users can find the exact tool for their intellectual needs.
